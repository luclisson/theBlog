### Plot

Do **not** read the following lines if you do not want to know about the storyline, as I will briefly summarize the story of Santiago, the protagonist.

Santiago's parents want him to become a priest, so they send him to a seminary. However, he realizes that his dream is to travel, and he wants to become a shepherd since shepherds travel the world with their flocks. The story is set in Andalusia and follows Santiago, who dreams twice about a treasure near the pyramids. He visits a woman who reads palms but learns little new until an old man joins him. This man is a wise king who gives Santiago two stones to help him make decisions. The king convinces him to undertake the long journey from Spain to Egypt as it is his life’s path.

Santiago travels by ship to Africa, where he is deceived by a thief and loses all the money he earned from selling his sheep. He works for over a year at a crystal merchant's shop and then sets out to cross the desert to reach the pyramids. On his journey, war breaks out, and his travel group takes refuge in an oasis. In the oasis, Santiago meets the love of his life, a young desert woman named Fatima. He also has a vision of the oasis being attacked by warriors. He warns the tribal leaders and meets the Alchemist, who teaches him the secrets of alchemy. Together, they continue on the path to Santiago's treasure. Despite many obstacles, Santiago eventually finds his treasure, a chest of gold, and sets off to reunite with his wife and embark on their next life journey together.

### Detailed Insights

_(unordered)_

1. **Love should not limit you**  
    If love hinders you from fulfilling your life’s dreams, it is not love. True love from another person should support your goals and not prevent you from achieving them.
    
2. **Live in the moment**  
    By looking at the present moment and your current situation rationally and without bias, you can reflect on and improve it. Improving the present automatically impacts your future positively.
    
3. **Why worry about the future?**  
    If it is about positive surprises, you can embrace them and be joyful. However, if it involves bad news, it was likely announced through a long process. To avoid this, you can refer to point 2 and possibly prevent unpleasant surprises. This method only works for things you can influence yourself.
    
4. **Do not let people influence your life with their opinions**  
    People from our close environment become important to us and a part of our lives. However, they may also want to play a role in our lives and influence us. If you do not reciprocate this influence or have different plans, it could lead to frustration, as they believe they know better and have a vision of how we should live our lives.
    
5. **A dream is essential for the joy of life**  
    You should think about potential life goals and hold on to them. Do not fear achieving them, as the process of reaching a dream allows for reflection and personal growth. This way, you become a better version of yourself and can pursue your next dream, ultimately becoming the best version of yourself.
